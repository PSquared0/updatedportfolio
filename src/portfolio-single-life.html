Got Mail?

Building a scalable email system to keep an insurance product top-of-mind.

Scroll down

Project Overview

The Problem

Insurance is a low-engagement category — customers don't think about their coverage until something goes wrong. Kasasa needed an email communication system that could stay relevant across the full customer lifecycle: acquisition, onboarding, retention, and re-engagement. One-off campaigns weren't enough. They needed a system.

What I Did

  • Designed a template architecture flexible enough to serve multiple campaign types and product lines without rebuilding from scratch
  • Established a content hierarchy that balanced product information with engagement — leading with value, not features
  • Created a visual system that maintained brand consistency across every stage of the customer lifecycle
  • Optimized layouts for desktop and mobile rendering across major email clients
  • Placed calls-to-action at moments of highest engagement within the read flow, not just at the bottom

Outcome

A reusable email communication system that reduced production time for new campaigns, kept brand standards consistent at every touchpoint, and gave the marketing team a scalable asset they could adapt across product lines without starting over each time.
