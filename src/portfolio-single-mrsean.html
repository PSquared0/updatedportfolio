Project Overview

The Problem

The client had no digital presence and relied entirely on referrals to find new students. Without a website, there was no way for parents to discover him, evaluate his teaching style, or take action — the business had a hard ceiling on growth.

What I Did

  • Mapped the parent discovery journey to identify what information drives enrollment decisions
  • Defined content structure and information architecture before any visual design began
  • Wrote and refined all copy for tone, clarity, and search discoverability
  • Built responsive layouts for desktop and mobile with accessibility standards met
  • Created a visual system that balances professional credibility with the playful nature of children's music education

Outcome

The client now has a professional platform he can manage independently, with a clear path from discovery to contact for prospective parents. The site removed the referral bottleneck and opened a direct channel for new student acquisition. Visit the Website →
